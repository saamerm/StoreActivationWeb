Frequently Asked Questions

Does the customer need to download an app?

No. This is a web-based experience. It works instantly on any smartphone browser via QR scan, reducing friction and increasing conversion rates.

How long does setup take?

Once we have your brand assets (logo, overlay design), a campaign can be live in under 24 hours. You receive the QR codes ready for print immediately.

Is the platform compliant with brand guidelines?

Yes. You have 100% control over the visual interface, photo overlays, and copy. It serves as a white-label extension of your brand.

Can we capture customer data?

Yes. You can require email or SMS opt-in to enter the prize drawing, allowing you to build your CRM for future retargeting.
